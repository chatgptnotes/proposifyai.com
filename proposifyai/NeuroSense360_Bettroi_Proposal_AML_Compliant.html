Project Overview

Development of a secure, analytics-driven web application for the NeuroSense360 Brain Health Platform — a part of Limitless Brain Wellness (LBW-Neuro) initiative.

The MVP will serve three user roles: Super Admin, Clinic Admin, and Patient Portal. The platform will include clinical dashboards, EEG data management, qEEG Pro reports, NeuroSense algorithms, and Stripe-integrated monetization.

Scope of Work

A. Landing Page

  • Informational content (videos, guides, articles on brain health)
  • Clinic locator with enquiry form (auto-detect location)
  • Paid brain scan purchase prompt linking to care plan page

B. Super Admin Dashboard

  • Multi-clinic control, agreements, and co-branding
  • Revenue analytics and report tracking
  • Notification alerts and full data access
  • Create/edit/deactivate clinic accounts
  • Upload EEG report PDF files per patient (manual upload)
  • View report usage stats per clinic
  • Grant/restrict trial access and monitor subscriptions

C. Clinic Admin Dashboard

  • Secure clinic login and authentication
  • Patient profile creation and management (name, age, gender, notes)
  • Upload and tracking of EEG (.edf) files (view-only)
  • View/download patient reports (PDF)
  • Track number of reports used
  • Clinic-specific analytics and restricted access
  • Automated trial/usage alerts

D. Patient Portal

  • Personal dashboard with history, care plans, and downloads
  • NeuroSense reports and progress tracking
  • Access to educational content and follow-up reminders

E. Algorithm Integration Layer

  • NeuroSense Equations for parameters: Cognition, Focus, Stress, Burnout, Creativity, Emotional Regulation, Learning
  • Scoring system with color-coded visual meters (Red-Yellow-Green)
  • Integration of 2 proprietary algorithms into report generation workflow

F. Payments & Monetization

  • Stripe integration for "per 10 reports" clinic bundle purchases
  • Usage analytics, alerts, and automated report unlocks
  • Unlock new report units on successful Stripe payment
