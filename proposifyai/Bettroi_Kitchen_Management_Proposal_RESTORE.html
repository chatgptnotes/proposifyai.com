3. Project Overview

The AI-Integrated Kitchen Management System is a comprehensive, modular platform designed to optimize restaurant and food service operations using artificial intelligence. The system streamlines core functions such as client order management, inventory tracking, recipe costing, and supplier monitoring while incorporating advanced AI-driven features for production planning, cost optimization, and financial forecasting.

Project Scope

We understand that the client requires an AI-integrated kitchen management system with a modular architecture to streamline inventory tracking, order processing, recipe costing, and supplier management. The system should leverage AI for real-time stock monitoring, automated procurement, cost optimization, and production planning. Additionally, advanced modules will enhance financial forecasting, logistics optimization, energy and labor cost management, and waste reduction.

4. Scope of Work

The project will be delivered in three distinct phases over 6 months, with each phase building upon the previous to create a comprehensive, enterprise-grade kitchen management solution.

PHASE 1: Core System Development (Sprints 1-4)

Client Order Management

  • Custom Recipe Creation: Allow clients to create and modify recipes with customizable ingredients and instructions
  • Order Processing: Track and manage customer orders, associate them with recipes, and update inventory levels accordingly
  • Order History & Reporting: Maintain detailed records of past orders for performance analysis

Inventory Management

  • Stock Tracking: AI-powered tracking using image recognition and barcode scanning
  • Automatic Reordering: Trigger procurement suggestions when stock falls below thresholds
  • Inventory Adjustments: Manual adjustments for lost, spoiled, or unaccounted inventory
  • Real-Time Stock Levels: Display real-time inventory data for efficient decision-making
  • Expiry Tracking: Track product shelf life and set expiration notifications

Recipe Costing & Batch Management

  • Ingredient Costing: Real-time cost tracking based on market price fluctuations
  • Batch Tracking: Track ingredients and costs per batch for exact cost per portion
  • Cost vs. Profit Alerts: Alerts when recipes become unprofitable due to rising costs

Supplier Price Monitoring

  • Smart Supplier Selection: AI-driven analysis based on price, delivery time, and quality
  • Automatic Purchase Orders: Generate orders based on inventory levels and pricing
  • Supplier Performance Tracking: Maintain history for analysis and future selection

Production Planning & Scheduling

  • Automated Task Assignment: Assign kitchen staff tasks based on orders and recipe requirements
  • Production Forecasting: Predict demand based on historical data and seasonal trends
  • Resource Allocation: Optimal allocation of kitchen resources including staff and ingredients

Profitability Analysis

  • Real-Time Cost Monitoring: Track recipe costs and compare with profit margins
  • Profitability Dashboard: Visual dashboard showing financial health of recipes and menus
  • Cost Alerts: Notify users when recipe costs become unsustainable

7. Development Methodology

Bettroi will implement an Agile methodology to ensure flexibility, collaboration, and efficiency throughout development. By breaking the project into iterative sprints, we will deliver functional components incrementally while incorporating continuous stakeholder feedback.

Agile Principles

  • Dedicated Product Owner: Aligns development with business goals
  • Sprint Planning & Execution: Prioritized features delivered incrementally
  • Daily Stand-Ups: Ensures clear communication and quick issue resolution
  • Real-Time Tracking: Monitored via tools like JIRA for transparency
  • Continuous Testing & Reviews: Guarantees security and high-quality performance

Sprint Structure (2 Weeks Each)

1. Project Initiation

  • Kickoff meeting with stakeholders
  • Product backlog creation
  • Sprint planning session
  • Define roles and responsibilities

2. Sprint Execution

  • Design and development of features
  • Daily stand-up meetings
  • Continuous testing and integration
  • Code reviews and quality checks

3. Sprint Review

  • Demonstrate completed features
  • Gather stakeholder feedback
  • Review sprint goals achievement
  • Plan necessary adjustments

4. Sprint Retrospective

  • Reflect on what worked well
  • Identify areas for improvement
  • Update processes and workflows
  • Prepare for next sprint
